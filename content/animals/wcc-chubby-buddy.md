# WCC Chubby Buddy (FB16111)

WCC Chubby Buddy dropped jaws coming in off **7,000+ acres of open west Utah desert range as an 1,100+ lb yearling.** He was hastily feed-bunker broke, "show cut" de-horned, and halter broke just in time to win **Senior Champion Bull at the 2014 Houston Livestock Show** — with the **largest ribeye and lowest backfat of the entire show.** A few weeks later he became the sale-record highest-selling bull of all time at the 2014 Texas Wagyu sale.

He's a freakishly big-framed, high-libido bull: he successfully bred a registered Angus cow at **under 7 months of age**, before being separated for weaning. He has matured into a tall, long, extreme-frame Akaushi — we have not seen another Akaushi bull with a frame as big as his anywhere in the AWA, easily exceeding 2,000 lbs at turnout.

## 2014 Houston Livestock Show — official ultrasound results

- **Back fat:** 0.13 in — *very lean; lowest in the show*
- **Marble Score:** 7.71 — *High Choice / Low Prime*
- **Ribeye Area (REA):** 17.25 in — *largest in the show*
